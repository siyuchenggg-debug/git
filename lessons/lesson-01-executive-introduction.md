# 第一课：高管自我介绍与中亚项目推介

## 教学目标

学员能够根据不同场景完成 30 秒、90 秒和 3 分钟介绍，并从“我是谁”自然过渡到“我们能为项目创造什么价值”。

## 90 分钟流程

1. 10 分钟：诊断式即兴介绍并录音；
2. 15 分钟：高管介绍的四段结构；
3. 15 分钟：职位、职责和项目角色表达；
4. 15 分钟：中信设计与第七设计院介绍；
5. 20 分钟：三种真实场景角色扮演；
6. 10 分钟：纠错、重说和对比录音；
7. 5 分钟：布置课后任务。

## 高管介绍四段结构

1. **Identity**：姓名、职务和机构；
2. **Responsibility**：分管业务；
3. **Relevance**：与本次项目直接相关的经验和角色；
4. **Purpose**：本次会谈希望推动的结果。

## 30 秒版本

> Good morning. I’m the Deputy Director of CITIC Design’s Seventh Design Institute. I am responsible for business development and strategy-to-planning integration. I have been closely involved in our Central Asian projects, particularly the Sairan CBD project in Almaty. I look forward to discussing how our teams can work together.

## 90 秒版本

> Good morning. I’m the Deputy Director of CITIC Design’s Seventh Design Institute. I am mainly responsible for business development and integrated strategy and planning services.
>
> My work focuses on identifying project opportunities, understanding clients’ development objectives, and coordinating our planning, urban design and architectural teams. I have also been closely involved in developing our business in Central Asia, including the Sairan CBD Manhattan urban design project in Almaty.
>
> I’m very pleased to meet you today. I hope we can better understand your priorities, share our initial thinking and explore a practical model for long-term cooperation.

## 从个人过渡到企业和项目

> Perhaps I could begin with a brief introduction to CITIC Design and our role in the project.

> In addition to my business development responsibilities, I coordinate the early-stage strategy and design resources required for projects of this scale.

> This brings me to the Sairan CBD project, which we see as the starting point for a broader partnership in Central Asia.

## 常见追问

### What exactly are you responsible for?

> I am responsible for business development, client engagement and the coordination of our strategy, planning and design resources.

### What is your role in this project?

> My role is to align the client’s development objectives with our technical proposal and to coordinate the commercial and technical workstreams.

### What makes your team different?

> Our strength lies in connecting early-stage development strategy with spatial planning, urban design and implementation. This helps reduce the gap between an attractive concept and a viable project.

## 场景练习

1. 在正式汇报前与阿拉木图市政府代表首次见面；
2. 与当地设计院管理层交流合作模式；
3. 向开发企业决策人介绍中信设计及后续服务。

## 课后任务

- 提交一段 90 秒录音；
- 准备三个本人实际负责过的商务工作实例；
- 用英文回答：`Why should a Central Asian client choose CITIC Design?`
