Generate Lesson 01 materials.

Lesson 01 theme:

Executive Introduction, Company Profile and Project Roadmap

Core scenario:

CITIC Design’s Seventh Design Institute meets Almaty-related institutions for the first time. The learner needs to introduce personal role, company capability, team strengths and Central Asian project experience.

Create the following files:

1. `lessons/lesson-01-executive-introduction.md`
2. `teacher-notes/lesson-01-teacher-notes.md`
3. `handouts/lesson-01-handout.md`
4. `listening/lesson-01-listening-script.md`
5. `listening/lesson-01-listening-task-sheet.md`
6. `roleplays/lesson-01-rolecards.md`
7. `slides/lesson-01-slide-outline.md`
8. `phrase-bank/lesson-01-phrase-bank.md`

Required outputs inside the lesson:

- 90-second executive self-introduction
- 2-minute company/team introduction
- 2-minute project roadmap explanation
- listening script where a foreign representative asks about who CITIC Design is, what the Seventh Design Institute does, and what role it plays in the Almaty project
- controlled speaking drills
- role-play for first meeting with Almaty-related institutions
- transfer task for AI Park, EXPO or Shymkent project introduction

Teaching style:

Follow the loop:

Warm-up → Listening Input → Listening Check → Language Focus → Controlled Speaking → Role-play → Feedback → Homework

Keep the tone professional, practical and suitable for a senior executive learner.
