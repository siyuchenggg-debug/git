## 高频洽谈表达

### 发现需求

> How does this project fit into the city’s broader development strategy?

> Which requirements are fixed, and which remain open for discussion?

> What would constitute a successful outcome from your perspective?

### 提出方案

> Our concept is structured around three key moves.

> The proposal balances commercial value with long-term public benefit.

> Our approach connects early-stage strategy with spatial planning and implementation.

### 确认分工

> We propose that CITIC Design lead the overall concept and urban design strategy.

> The local partner would be responsible for statutory compliance and local submissions.

> Could we jointly prepare a responsibility matrix before the next stage begins?

### 谈范围与条件

> Our fee covers one preferred concept and two rounds of consolidated revisions.

> We can accommodate the request, provided that the scope and programme are adjusted accordingly.

> Any material change to the approved brief may affect both the fee and the schedule.

### 推动长期合作

> We see this project not as a one-off assignment, but as the starting point for a long-term partnership.

> Could we agree on a roadmap for the next three opportunities?

> Let me summarize the decisions, open issues and responsible parties.
